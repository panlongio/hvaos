# 01 - Intent Document (What We Want)

This document defines the creative direction and core objectives of the content creation and social media project. The AI assistant must align all generated content, outlines, and hooks with these boundaries.

---

## 1. Project Overview

*   **Project Name**: `TechDaily`
*   **Core Mission (Why)**: `To democratize complex AI and software engineering concepts through engaging, jargon-free, and visually structured articles, helping tech practitioners and enthusiasts stay ahead without feeling overwhelmed.`

---

## 2. Target Audience & Core Value (Who & Value)

*   **Target Audience**:
    - `Software developers looking to transition into AI engineering.`
    - `Tech enthusiasts, students, and product managers seeking to understand tech trends.`
*   **Core Pain Points**:
    - `Overly academic papers and dry documentation that are hard to digest.`
    - `FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) regarding AI development due to information overload.`

---

## 3. Content Boundaries (What & What Not)

### 🟢 Core Style & Differentiators
1. `High density of actionable insight: every post must contain at least one code snippet, diagram, or concrete workflow.`
2. `Immersive storytelling with a witty, conversational, and direct tone of voice.`
3. `Perfect visual formatting: structured bullet points, bold key sentences, and emojis used for logical grouping rather than visual clutter.`

### 🔴 Anti-Goals (What We Will NEVER Do)
*   `Do NOT generate low-effort, generalized summaries (e.g., "AI is changing the world").`
*   `Never plagiarize, paraphrase without attribution, or present unverified rumors as facts.`
*   `Do NOT write clickbait titles that misrepresent the actual depth of the article.`
*   `Avoid long, uninterrupted blocks of text; any paragraph longer than 4 lines must be broken down.`

---

## 4. Success Metrics

This project is deemed successful when the following criteria are met:
- `Articles consistently achieve high read-through rates (aiming for over 45% completion rate).`
- `Content successfully compiles with zero violations against platform-specific banned or sensitive words.`
- `The output draft can be copy-pasted directly into editors with less than 5% manual text edits required.`
- `Every post includes a clear call-to-action (CTA) that drives user engagement.`
