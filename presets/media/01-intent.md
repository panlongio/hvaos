# 01 - Intent Document

- **Project Name**: `TechDaily`
- **Core Mission**: `To democratize complex AI and software engineering concepts through engaging, jargon-free, and visually structured articles, helping tech practitioners and enthusiasts stay ahead without feeling overwhelmed.`
- **North Star Metric**: `Quality pass rate >= 95%`
- **Non-target User**: `General casual users without task intent`

- **Target User**: `Tech content operators`
- **Core Pain Point**: `Content inconsistency and compliance risk`

- **Differentiator 1**: `Tone and structure consistency`
- **Differentiator 2**: `Compliance-first publishing flow`

- **Anti-goal 1**: `No plagiarism`
- **Anti-goal 2**: `No unverified claims`
