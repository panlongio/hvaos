## 3. Failed Attempts & Retrospective Lessons

> [!WARNING]
> **AI Assistant Alert: Do NOT recommend or reintroduce the following systems, as they have failed to yield results.**
> 
*   **Attempt 1: Strict Hour-by-Hour Micro-scheduling**
    - *Method tried*: Allocated every single minute of the day with zero gaps, including meals and transport.
    - *Failure reason*: Led to extreme stress, zero tolerance for unexpected delays, and rapid abandonment of the schedule.
    - *Conclusion*: Always include a 15-30% buffer block daily. Use task lists for small tasks instead of blocking specific minutes.
*   **Attempt 2: Manual Pen-and-Paper Expense Tracking**
    - *Method tried*: Writing down every single transaction manually on a physical notepad.
    - *Failure reason*: High friction led to forgotten logs, resulting in massive gaps in monthly budget analyses.
    - *Conclusion*: Always use digital tools or automatic export scripts to minimize input friction and ensure completeness.
