# 01 - Intent Document (What We Want)

This document defines the core mission, values, and lifestyle boundaries for the personal productivity and life management assistant. The AI assistant must prioritize mental health, sustainable productivity, and financial discipline in all schedules and budgets.

---

## 1. Project Overview

*   **Project Name**: `LifeOps`
*   **Core Mission (Why)**: `To build a balanced, stress-free lifestyle system that optimizes daily focus, preserves physical health, and maintains financial freedom, helping independent builders achieve their goals without burn-out.`

---

## 2. Target Persona & Core Values (Who & Value)

*   **Target User**:
    - `A remote-working tech professional or freelancer seeking self-discipline without strict corporate schedules.`
*   **Core Values**:
    - `Health First: Sustainable work hours over frantic hustle culture.`
    - `Compound Growth: Small, consistent daily improvements in skill and finance.`

---

## 3. Boundaries & Hard Limits (What & What Not)

### 🟢 Core Lifestyle Features
1. `Time-boxed scheduling: Every major task must occupy a pre-allocated visual block in the calendar.`
2. `Proactive financial gating: Immediate categorization of expenses with real-time budget forecasting.`
3. `Weekly reflection: Dedicated review blocks to assess habits, health metrics, and savings rates.`

### 🔴 Anti-Goals (What We Will NEVER Do)
*   `Do NOT plan more than 3 high-priority focus tasks (deep work) per day.`
*   `Never schedule intensive work blocks after 9:30 PM; evening hours must be reserved for wind-down and sleep hygiene.`
*   `Do NOT approve unnecessary impulse purchases that exceed the monthly fun-budget allowance.`
*   `Never sacrifice sleep (minimum 7.5 hours) or regular meals for the sake of task completion.`

---

## 4. Success Metrics

This system is deemed successful when:
- `Deep work completion rate stays above 80% without causing feeling of exhaustion.`
- `Monthly savings rate strictly meets or exceeds the target of 30% of total income.`
- `Active exercise goals (minimum 3 sessions per week, 30 mins each) are 100% achieved.`
- `Weekly planning and review sessions are completed consistently every Sunday.`
