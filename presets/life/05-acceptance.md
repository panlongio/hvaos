# 05 - Acceptance Document (Quality Gates & Health Baselines)

This document is the checklist used to verify whether proposed calendars, budgets, or travel plans are safe and optimized for lifestyle balance.

---

## 1. Safety Gates & Pre-checks

The AI assistant must verify the following items before submitting any proposed plan:
- **Double Booking Check**: Run a calendar conflict check. Ensure no two events overlap.
- **Budget Margin Verification**: Confirm that new expense proposals leave a minimum 10% safety cushion in their respective monthly categories.
- **Travel Transit Buffers**: For travel itineraries, ensure transit times include an extra 50% buffer to account for delays (e.g. 3 hours before international flights instead of 2).

---

## 2. Personal UX & Focus Baselines

*   **Mindfulness Blocks**: Ensure each day contains at least 30 minutes of unstructured "white space" for rest and contemplation.
*   **Logical Task Grouping**: Admin tasks (emails, bills, shopping) must be batched together in single blocks rather than scattered throughout the day.
*   **Readable Summary**: Proposed changes must be summarized in a simple, bulleted message containing: (1) what changed, (2) financial impact, (3) time impact.

---

## 3. Weekly Boundaries

*   **Maximum Focus Time**: Total scheduled focus/deep-work blocks must not exceed 5 hours per day to prevent brain fatigue.
*   **Minimum Sleep Window**: Ensure the sleep block is strictly 8 hours on the calendar, protecting the 10:30 PM - 6:30 AM window.

---

## 4. Negative Gatekeeper (Strictly Unacceptable Outcomes)

> [!CAUTION]
> **If any of the following flaws exist in the proposal, it will be rejected and sent back for recalculation:**
> 
- `Any proposal that exposes unmasked private keys, bank accounts, or identity numbers.`
- `Proposals that reduce nightly sleep slots below 7 hours.`
- `Suggesting recurring subscription services without providing a free alternative first.`
- `Overwriting locked calendar blocks (e.g., gym blocks or family time) without explicit prior approval.`
